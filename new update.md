## English

### About SPMAPI

SPMAPI Private Limited is a registered land survey and measurement firm based in Bihar. Our mission is to digitize property boundaries and provide accurate land mapping solutions that help resolve and prevent land disputes among families, neighbors, and property owners.

We combine modern surveying techniques with professional expertise to deliver precise land measurement, boundary verification, and mapping services across Bihar.

---

### Our Services

#### 1. Land Measurement (Jameen Mapi)

Accurate measurement of land parcels to determine exact property dimensions and boundaries.

#### 2. Land Demarcation

Identification and marking of legal property boundaries on the ground to prevent future disputes and encroachments.

#### 3. Map Request & Survey Support

Assistance in obtaining official land maps and assigning certified Amin (Surveyors) for professional property measurement.

#### 4. Property Boundary Verification

Verification of existing property boundaries using official records and field surveys.

#### 5. Digital Land Mapping

Creation of digital maps and property records for better land management and documentation.

---

### Why Choose SPMAPI?

* Professional and certified survey services
* Accurate land measurement
* Modern mapping technology
* Transparent process
* Quick service delivery
* Support throughout the survey process
* Assistance in resolving boundary-related disputes

### हमारी सेवाएँ

#### 1. जमीन मापी (Land Measurement)

भूमि की सही लंबाई, चौड़ाई और क्षेत्रफल का सटीक मापन।

#### 2. भूमि सीमांकन (Demarcation)

भूमि की कानूनी सीमाओं को चिन्हित करना ताकि भविष्य में विवाद और अतिक्रमण से बचा जा सके।

#### 3. नक्शा एवं सर्वे सहायता

आधिकारिक भूमि नक्शा प्राप्त करने तथा प्रमाणित अमीन की नियुक्ति में सहायता।

#### 4. भूमि सीमा सत्यापन

राजस्व अभिलेखों एवं स्थल निरीक्षण के आधार पर भूमि सीमा का सत्यापन।

#### 5. डिजिटल भूमि मानचित्रण

भूमि का डिजिटल नक्शा तैयार करना जिससे रिकॉर्ड प्रबंधन और दस्तावेज़ीकरण आसान हो सके।
