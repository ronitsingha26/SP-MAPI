SPMAPI Private Limited is a registered land survey and measurement firm based in Bihar that aims to digitize property boundaries. Its core mission is to provide accurate land mapping to help resolve and prevent property disputes among families and neighbors.

Services & Requests
The company offers a structured process for handling land measurements:
Land Measurement (Jameen Mapi): Assessing property boundaries to ensure correct dimensions.
Deforestation/Demarcation: Properly clearing and marking out land boundaries in their true, legal form.
Requesting Maps & Tools: Facilitating official map generation and assigning certified Amins (surveyors) to measure property lines.
